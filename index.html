boombot by TerrordactylDesigns

Chat bot and DJ bot for Turntable.fm

Need your own robot DJ? Want to welcome people to your room without typing? Want to impress the ladies? You want BOOMBOT.........

Dependencies

Node.js
NPM
https://github.com/alaingilbert/Turntable-API

Install

once you have node running, npm, and the ttapi installed:
Create a FB or twitter account for your new bot.
Log in as the bot and use this to get your bots uid, auth token, and your rooms id:
http://alaingilbert.github.com/Turntable-API/bookmarklet.html
Now use the same method to get your personal accounts user id for control of the bot
Open boombot.js in Sublime text 2 (Or your editor of choice... but really... go get sublime... its great..)
replace the xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx's with your auth, bot id, and your own personal user id.
each section that requires these are appropriately commented to tell you where to enter them.
There will also be a few places where you will replace the text YourUserName with your own so the bot will express its love for you.
Add to the arrays anything you want to add or change. Would be nice of you to keep the url to obtain your own copy under the /boombot command, but I won't hunt you down if you want it to go.
path to the saved location,
node boombot.js
