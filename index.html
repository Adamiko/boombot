boombot by TerrordactylDesigns

Chat bot and DJ bot for Turntable.fm

Need your own robot DJ? Want to welcome people to your room without typing? Want to auto tweet the current playing song? Want to impress the ladies? You want BOOMBOT.........

Dependencies

Node.js
NPM
ttapi - https://github.com/alaingilbert/Turntable-API
ntwitter - https://github.com/AvianFlu/ntwitter (If you want twitter integration)

Install

once you have node running, npm, and the ttapi installed:
Create a FB or twitter account for your new bot.
Log in as the bot and use this to get your bots uid, auth token, and your rooms id:
http://alaingilbert.github.com/Turntable-API/bookmarklet.html
Now use the same method to get your personal accounts user id for control of the bot
Open boombot.js in Sublime text 2 (Or your editor of choice... but really... go get sublime... its great..)
replace the xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx's in the variables section with your bots auth, bot id, and your own personal user id.
replace YourUserName with your own personal username (or whatever else you want the bot to call you) in the variables section.
Add to the arrays anything you want to add or change. Would be nice of you to keep the url to obtain your own copy under the /boombot command, but I won't hunt you down if you want it to go.
path to the saved location,
node boombot.js

Automated tweeting

This feature is commented out by default for people who could care less about twitter.
To use, read below and uncomment the twitter area at the bottom of the boombot.js file.
Create a twitter acct if you don't already have one.
Then go to dev.twitter.com and sign in with that acct and enable it for development.
Create a new twitter application. VERY IMPORTANT: BEFORE you request your Oauth tokens set the app to read + write or read + write + direct message.
Once you have your token keys, add them to the appropriately commented areas at the bottom of the file.
The bot will now tweet the current track and artist with a #Turntablefm hashtag.

Commands

Everyone:

  • /lyrics - returns lyrics snippet and link to full lyrics from lyrics wiki
  • /video - returns a link to a youtube video search for the current song
  • /hello - says hello back
  • /dance - gives you a dance partner
  • /boombot - information about the bot and how to acquire one
  • /help - list the commands
  • /rules - list the rooms rules
  • /cheer - cheer for the song
  • /boo - boo the song
  • like a boss - display a random like a boss meme image
  • /haters - display a random haters meme image
  • meow - random super troopers meow reference
  • /rich - probably my favorite Bender quote ever
  • /chuck - random Chuck Norris joke from the icndb API
  • /winning - random Charlie Sheen quote from the API

Administrator/"The Master"

These commands are given as: boombot <command>

  • djmode - make the bot get on stage and DJ
  • getdown - make the bot get off the stage
  • skip - make the bot skip the song it is playing
  • addsong - add the current playing song to the bots playlist
  • good - remind the bot he's a good boy. Maybe he will kill you last during the robot apocalypse.
  • dance - make the bot upvote **this feature is against the TOS of turntable.fm and is disabled**
  • voodoo up - switch the bot to the voodoo doll avatar (requires 100 DJ points)
  • kitten up - switch the bot to the green kitten avatar (requires 300 DJ points)
  • alien up - switch the bot to the alien avatar (requires 100 DJ points)
  • vampire up - switch the bot to the vampire avatar (requires 100 DJ points)
  • phone up - sets the bots laptop to the iPhone
  • fruit up - sets the bots laptop to Mac
  • nix up - sets the bots laptop to Linux/Ubuntu
  • chrome up - sets the bots laptop to ChromeOS
